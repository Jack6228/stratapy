Adjusting Assets
======================================

stratapy offers significant flexibility in adjust the lithologies, minerals, and features displayed on your stratigraphic logs. 

- Existing lithological patterns can be modified by changing the colour or label, while new patterns can be added from custom images, or single-colour fills can also be used.
- Minerals can be edited or added, customising the shapes, colours, and labels to suit your needs.
- Our large library of features can be tailored by changing their label or the section of the legend in which they appear. New features can also be created from custom images.
